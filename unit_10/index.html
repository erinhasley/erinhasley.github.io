On View, Now!

Water Changes; Water Changes Everything


Diane Hasley, Solo Exhibition


April 4, 2016 - May 31, 2016 | Evergreen Brick Works, 550 Bayview Avenue | Admission is free.


Installed in the Young Welcome Centre, this is an exhibit of over 40 encaustic works by Diane Hasley. Inspired by the changes that take place in and over Lake Ontario and the Southern Georgian Bay section of Lake Huron, the art on display reflects the transformation of water into waves, foam, mist, ice, snow, and cloud. Water takes on an array of colours; it sparkles in the sun; it can be still, full of motion, or turbulent. This installation is a tribute to the importance of water at a time when the connection between climate change and water is an ever-present concern. All proceeds will be donated to Evergreen.
